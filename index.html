About Me



Hi! My name is Anna Heselbarth. I am software developer and business owner. I have had the pleasure of being a stay at home mom for almost 12 years. Now that my little ones are all off to school, I decided to jump into a career that is interesting, exciting, and allowed me to always stay challenged which is something that I love.

How I came across programming and software development was when I decided to take my health coaching business and start an online portion of it. With that, I needed to create online courses, landing pages, email marketing systems, sales funnels, lead magnets, leads and customer database, track business expenses, profits, and return on investments. I also needed to be able to have a way to help track improvements and programs of each client. All of this lead me to see the importance of having simplified yet effective software to take care of all the needs of digitized businesses.

I wanted to learn more. How to actual code the projects needed to customize to my business. This is when Eleven Fifty Academy came across my path and I took an intro course to coding. I immediately fell in love with coding and signed up the next day.

After that, I dove into the different types of programming. I researched resources such as youtube tutorials, codeacademy, freecodecamp.org, codewars, coursera, udemy, and checked out every book the library had on coding. I knew this was the path that I was destined to do and like all things I do in life I jump in with both feet and learn as much as I can. Eleven Fifty Academy was by far my best resource with hands on projects and teachers helping along the way. They made me feel prepared to step into the world of software development and start this new journey in life.
